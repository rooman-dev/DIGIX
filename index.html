Our Story

The Genesis

DIGIX Lab was born from a vision to revolutionize digital education in Pakistan. Founded by Prof. Dr. Hameedur Rahman, our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a bridge between academic learning and industry demands in the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

The Launch

In partnership with Air University Business Incubation Center, DIGIX Lab opened its doors with state-of-the-art facilities and a team of passionate industry professionals. Our strategic location in Islamabad, Pakistan's capital, positioned us at the heart of the country's educational and technological ecosystem.

Growth & Impact

Since our establishment, DIGIX Lab has grown from a small training center to Pakistan's premier digital education hub. We've successfully trained over 500+ students, completed 50+ industry projects, and maintained an impressive 85% placement rate for our graduates.

Global Recognition

Today, DIGIX Lab stands as a beacon of innovation in digital education. Our graduates work with leading companies worldwide, our research contributes to international publications, and our training programs are recognized for their quality and industry relevance.
